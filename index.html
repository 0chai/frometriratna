Welcome to Frome Triratna Buddhist Community


Our sangha/meditation evening is on Thursdays, 7:30pm at St May’s church hall. If you are familiar with the Mindfulness of Breathing and Loving Kindness/Metta Bhavana meditations you are welcome.

If you are new to meditation we run occasional intro classes. Please email us on the address below., where we explore Buddhism as the heart of a living practice in a wide variety of ways.

Festivals, men’s days, women’s days, retreats, gatherings and celebrations - our small, vibrant, friendly community looks forward to meeting you!

For more information and to join our mailing list, please email frometriratna@gmail.com
